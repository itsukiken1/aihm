Cross-Border Sourcing Guide

How to Calculate Profit When Sourcing from 1688

Many sellers only look at the product price on 1688 and the selling price on Amazon. But the real margin depends on at least 6 cost factors: product cost (CNY converted to USD), international shipping (sea freight at $3-6/CBM or air at $4-8/kg), customs duties (0-28% depending on HTS code and Section 301 tariffs), platform fees (Amazon charges 15% referral + $3-6 FBA fee), storage costs, and return rates. A product that looks like it has 80% margins on 1688 often ends up at 20-35% after all costs. Use SourceCalc to calculate this directly on 1688 product pages, or BulkCalc to evaluate multiple products at once.

Shipping from China: Sea vs Air vs Express

Choosing the right shipping method can swing your profit margins by 6-10 percentage points. Sea freight (25-45 day transit) is cheapest at $3-15/CBM but requires larger volumes. Air freight (5-10 days) costs $4-8/kg and works well for lightweight or time-sensitive products. Express courier (DHL/FedEx, 3-5 days) costs $8-15/kg but is best for samples and small test batches under 100 kg. For European sellers, China-Europe rail freight (15-20 days, $3-5/kg) offers a middle ground. Compare all methods for your specific shipment with ShipCalc.

1688 vs Alibaba for Small Sellers

1688.com is China's domestic B2B wholesale marketplace, owned by Alibaba Group. The same factories sell on both platforms, but 1688 prices are typically 20-40% lower because there's no export markup. The tradeoff: 1688 is entirely in Chinese, requires Alipay for payment, and has no international buyer protection. For small sellers testing products with 50-200 unit batches, 1688's lower MOQs and prices often make it the better choice. For larger orders where you want Trade Assurance and English communication, Alibaba remains easier.

FAQ

Are these tools really free?

Yes. All AIHM tools are 100% free with no signup required. They run entirely in your browser — no data is sent to any server. Your product data stays on your device.

What selling platforms are supported?

Amazon FBA, Amazon FBM, Shopify, Temu, eBay, and TikTok Shop. Each platform has accurate fee calculations including referral fees, fulfillment fees, and transaction charges.

What countries can I ship to?

ShipCalc supports 15+ destination countries: USA, UK, Germany, France, Japan, Australia, Canada, UAE, Saudi Arabia, South Korea, Singapore, Netherlands, Spain, Italy, and Brazil. Each with specific freight rates for sea, air, express, and rail shipping.

How accurate are the shipping cost estimates?

ShipCalc uses industry-standard rate ranges updated periodically. Actual rates vary by freight forwarder, season, and shipment specifics. The estimates are designed for product evaluation and budgeting — always get formal quotes from 2-3 freight forwarders before committing to a shipment.
